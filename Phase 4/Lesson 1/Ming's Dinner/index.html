Welcome To Ming's Dinner


Ming's Dinner brand has its origins in a small canteen and a cafe in a cinema theatre called Inox in Hyderabad in the Year 1999.

A small 100-seater restaurant started serving typical Hyderabadi cuisine like Biryani etc. from early 20s. The cafe also used to sell general food items to the people as a one-stop food store.

Now Ming's Dinner expanded it operations in more than 11 cities.
